Supplementary Videos for Vehicle and Electric Scooter Interaction Research

This webpage contains the videos of qualitative simulations from the paper "Sustainability Evaluation in Vehicle and Electric Scooter Interaction," which is accepted by the 27th IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITSC 2024).

Use Case 1: Intersection Scenario

Vehicle Controlled by CAP without V2X

This video demonstrates a collision when the ego vehicle (vehicle #3) is under the Collision Avoidance Planner (CAP) control but without the Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X) communication module.

Vehicle Controlled by CAP with V2X

This video demonstrates a collision when the CAP controls the ego vehicle with assistance from the V2X unit.

Vehicle Controlled by MPC

This video demonstrates no collision due to advanced MPC control implementation.

Use Case 2: Straight Road Scenario

Vehicle Controlled by CAP without V2X

This video demonstrates a collision when the ego vehicle is controlled by the developed CAP without V2X.

Vehicle Controlled by CAP with V2X

This video demonstrates improved safety when V2X is integrated and warns the ego vehicle in advance.

Vehicle Controlled by MPC

This video demonstrates no collision due to advanced MPC control.
